## ğŸ’¡ Tips for Success

### Application Strategy
- **Quality over Quantity**: Better to send 5 great applications than 20 mediocre ones
- **Customize Everything**: Tailor resume and cover letter for each application
- **Follow Up**: Send polite follow-up after 1 week of no response
- **Track Metrics**: Monitor what works and double down

### Networking Strategy
- **Be Genuine**: Build real relationships, not just transactional connections
- **Provide Value**: Share insights, help others, engage authentically
- **Leverage Alumni**: Reach out to ITS Surabaya graduates at target companies
- **Referrals are Gold**: Referrals have 5x higher success rate

### Interview Strategy
- **Prepare Stories**: Have 10 STAR stories ready for behavioral questions
- **Practice Live**: Do mock interviews with friends or online platforms
- **Ask Questions**: Show genuine interest in company and role
- **Follow Up**: Send thank-you email within 24 hours

### Mental Health
- **Be Patient**: Remote job search can take 2-3 months
- **Stay Positive**: Rejection is normal, learn from each experience
- **Celebrate Wins**: Track small victories (applications, responses, interviews)
- **Take Breaks**: Don't burn out, maintain work-life balance

## ğŸ†˜ Troubleshooting

### Low Response Rate (< 5%)
**Possible Issues**:
- Resume not ATS-optimized
- Applying to wrong roles
- Cover letter too generic
- LinkedIn profile incomplete

**Solutions**:
- Run resume through ATS scanner
- Focus on better-fit roles
- Customize cover letters more
- Optimize LinkedIn profile

### No Interview Invitations
**Possible Issues**:
- Not enough applications
- Weak portfolio/GitHub
- Lack of referrals
- Targeting too senior roles

**Solutions**:
- Increase application volume
- Improve portfolio projects
- Network more actively
- Apply to broader range of roles

### Interviews But No Offers
**Possible Issues**:
- Technical skills gaps
- Poor communication
- Weak behavioral answers
- Salary expectations mismatch

**Solutions**:
- More LeetCode practice
- Mock interviews
- Prepare better STAR stories
- Research market rates
