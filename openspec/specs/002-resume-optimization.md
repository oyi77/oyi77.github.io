## ğŸ¨ Design Guidelines

### ATS Optimization
- **Font**: Arial, Calibri, or Helvetica (ATS-friendly)
- **Font Size**: 10-11pt body, 12-14pt headers
- **Margins**: 0.5-0.75 inches
- **Sections**: Clear headers (EXPERIENCE, EDUCATION, SKILLS)
- **Bullet Points**: Use standard bullets (â€¢), not fancy symbols
- **Keywords**: Include job description keywords naturally
- **No Graphics**: Avoid images, charts, tables (ATS can't parse)
- **File Format**: PDF (preserve formatting) + Plain Text (ATS backup)

### Visual Hierarchy
- **Name**: Largest, bold
- **Contact Info**: Smaller, one line
- **Section Headers**: Bold, uppercase, slightly larger
- **Company/Role**: Bold
- **Dates**: Right-aligned or in parentheses
- **Achievements**: Bullet points with bold numbers

### Quantification
- Always include numbers: percentages, dollar amounts, team sizes
- Use bold for key metrics: **90%+ win rate**, **$100K profit**
- Compare before/after: "Reduced costs by **40%**"
- Show scale: "**100M+ monthly events**", "**3,000+ employees**"
